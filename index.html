Seven Wonders of the World

Louvre Paris

France

One of the world's most renowned museums, housed in a historic palace. Home to an extensive collection of art and artifacts, including the iconic Mona Lisa. A cultural treasure trove that showcases centuries of human creativity and history. A must-visit destination for art enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

Mt. Fuji

Japan

A majestic and iconic volcanic mountain located southwest of Tokyo. Standing at 3,776 meters, it is Japan's highest peak. Known for its symmetrical cone shape and snow-capped summit, Mt. Fuji is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a popular destination for climbers and nature enthusiasts. Its beauty and spiritual significance make it a symbol of Japan's natural splendor.

El Nido

Philippines

A tropical paradise nestled in the Palawan province. With its crystal-clear turquoise waters, limestone cliffs, and pristine white sand beaches, El Nido is a haven for beach lovers and island adventurers. Explore hidden lagoons, vibrant coral reefs, and stunning karst landscapes.

Kita-Kamakura

Japan

A charming historic town located south of Tokyo, known for its numerous temples and shrines, including the famous Zeniarai Benzaiten Ugafuku Shrine. A popular destination for those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, offering serene nature trails, traditional architecture, and a glimpse into Japan's rich cultural heritage.

La Palma

Spain

A volcanic island in the Canary Islands archipelago known for its rugged, natural beauty, including lush forests, black sand beaches, and towering cliffs. A paradise for hikers and stargazers, offering breathtaking views of the night sky and stunning landscapes at every turn.

Banff

Canada

Majestic Rocky Mountain town known for its stunning natural beauty, including turquoise lakes, towering peaks, and abundant wildlife. A paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, offering hiking, skiing, and breathtaking vistas at every turn.
